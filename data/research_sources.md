## Access Notes

- **Paywall sources** (Statista, Bloomberg, DigiTimes, HBR): Brave Search can often extract key stats from snippets. Use `web_search` — the summary often contains the data point needed.
- **Bot-restricted** (MacroTrends, Grokipedia): Block automated fetchers (402/403) but work in browsers. Use `web_search` to find data snippets instead of `fetch_url`.
- **WikiChip**: Intermittent 520 errors. Retry or use cached data. Alternative: WikiChip Fuse (fuse.wikichip.org).
- **API Ninjas Premium**: Required for `earningstranscript`. Falls back to Motley Fool transcripts via `fetch_url`.
- **Company IR pages**: Always check for direct PDF links to annual reports and earnings presentations.
- **Substack/blog sources**: Generally accessible, but some may move or go inactive. Check dates.

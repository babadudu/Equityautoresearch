## Anat Ashkenazi (CFO, Alphabet) & Philipp Schindler (CBO, Google) — Financial Metrics, Cloud Margins, CapEx Escalation, Advertising AI ROI

This atom collects direct quotes from Alphabet's CFO Anat Ashkenazi and Google's Chief Business Officer Philipp Schindler, covering Cloud profitability trajectory, CapEx guidance revisions, shareholder returns, and AI-driven advertising performance metrics.

---

## Anat Ashkenazi — CFO, Alphabet

### Google Cloud Operating Margin Trajectory

**Context:** Q1 2025 Earnings Call (April 2025), reporting Cloud margin expansion as a key proof point of the segment's profitability inflection.

> "Operating margin increased from 9.4% to 17.8%."

— Source: [Q1 2025 Earnings Call, Apr 2025](https://www.rev.com/transcripts/alphabet-q1-2025-earnings-call)

**Context:** Q2 2025 Earnings Call (July 2025), confirming continued Cloud margin expansion through mid-year.

> "Google Cloud operating margin increased from 11.3% to 20.7%."

> "YouTube advertising revenues increased 13% to $9.8 billion, driven by direct response advertising."

> "Google Cloud backlog increased 18% sequentially in Q2 and 38% year over year, reaching $106 billion."

> "Depreciation increased $1.3 billion year over year to $5 billion, reflecting a growth rate of 35%."

— Source: [Q2 2025 Earnings Call, Jul 2025](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/07/23/alphabet-googl-q2-2025-earnings-call-transcript/)

**Context:** Q4 2025 Earnings Call (February 2026), reporting Cloud's full-year profitability surge and backlog doubling.

> "Cloud operating income was $5.3 billion more than doubling year over year."

> "Operating margin increased from 17.5% in the fourth quarter of last year to 30.1%."

> "Google Cloud's backlog increased 55% sequentially and more than doubled year over year, reaching $240 billion."

— Source: [Q4 2025 Earnings Call, Feb 2026](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/04/alphabet-googl-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/)

---

### CapEx Guidance — Escalation from $75B to $175–185B

**Context:** Q1 2025 Earnings Call (April 2025), confirming the original 2025 CapEx plan.

> "We still expect to invest approximately $75 billion in CapEx this year."

> "We returned value to shareholders...in the form of $15.1 billion in share repurchases."

> "Our board declared a 5% increase in our quarterly dividend."

— Source: [Q1 2025 Earnings Call, Apr 2025](https://www.rev.com/transcripts/alphabet-q1-2025-earnings-call)

**Context:** Q2 2025 Earnings Call (July 2025), announcing the upward revision from $75B to $85B for 2025.

> "We now expect to invest approximately $85 billion in CapEx in 2025, up from $75 billion estimate."

— Source: [Q2 2025 Earnings Call, Jul 2025](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/07/23/alphabet-googl-q2-2025-earnings-call-transcript/)

**Context:** Q3 2025 Earnings Call (November 2025), responding to investor questions on return on capital investment discipline.

> "We have a rigorous framework and approach by which we evaluate these long-term investments."

— Source: [Q3 2025 Earnings Call, Nov 2025](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/11/27/alphabet-googl-q3-2025-earnings-call-transcript/)

**Context:** Q4 2025 Earnings Call (February 2026), announcing a doubling of CapEx guidance for 2026.

> "For the full year 2026, we expect CapEx to be in the range of $175 billion to $185 billion."

— Source: [Q4 2025 Earnings Call, Feb 2026](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/04/alphabet-googl-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/)

## Philipp Schindler — Chief Business Officer, Google

### AI-Driven Advertising ROI Metrics

**Context:** Q1 2024 Earnings Call (April 2024), articulating the link between AI model improvements and advertiser outcomes.

> "As advances accelerate in our underlying AI models, our ability to help businesses find users at speed and scale and drive ROI just keeps getting better."

— Source: [Q1 2024 Earnings Call, Apr 2024](https://blog.google/inside-google/message-ceo/alphabet-earnings-q1-2024/)

**Context:** Q1 2025 Earnings Call (April 2025), revealing Demand Gen campaign performance data.

> "Businesses using Demand Gen now see an average 26% year-on-year increase in conversions per dollar spent for goals like purchases and leads. And when using Demand Gen with product feed, on average they see more than double the conversion per dollar spent year-over-year."

— Source: [Q1 2025 Earnings Call, Apr 2025](https://ppc.land/google-q1-2025-earnings-reveal-advertising-strength-amid-ai-transformation/)

**Context:** Q2 2025 Earnings Call (July 2025), presenting incremental conversion lift data for AI Max and Smart Bidding Exploration tools.

> "Advertisers that activate AI Max in search campaigns typically see 14% more conversions."

> "Campaigns using Smart Bidding Exploration see a 19% increase in conversions on average."

— Source: [Q2 2025 Earnings Call, Jul 2025](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/07/23/alphabet-googl-q2-2025-earnings-call-transcript/)

## YouTube Shorts Monetization Parity

**Context:** Q2 2025 Earnings Call (July 2025), confirming that Shorts has achieved revenue-per-watch-hour parity with long-form in the US market.

> "In the US, Shorts now earn as much revenue per watch hour as traditional in-stream on YouTube."

— Source: [Q2 2025 Earnings Call, Jul 2025](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/07/23/alphabet-googl-q2-2025-earnings-call-transcript/)

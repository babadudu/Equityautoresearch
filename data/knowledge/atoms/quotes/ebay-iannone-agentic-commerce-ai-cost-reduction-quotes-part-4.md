## Jamie Iannone — Agentic Commerce, AI Cost Reduction & Competitive Moat Quotes

### Agentic Commerce & OpenAI Collaboration (2025)

On the January 2025 partnership with OpenAI to launch Agentic Commerce — allowing AI agents to autonomously search, compare prices, and place orders on behalf of buyers:

> "This agentic approach to ecommerce could create an entirely new channel for discovery and shopping online... we anticipate this collaboration could expand the reach of our sellers, giving more buyers exposure to eBay's unique and differentiated inventory"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([Digital Commerce 360, 2025-03-10](https://www.digitalcommerce360.com/2025/03/10/ebay-sales-revenue-gmv-q4-2024/))

---

### AI as a Cost-Reduction Weapon (2025)

Iannone frames friction — not Amazon or Temu — as eBay's true competitor, and AI as the primary tool to eliminate it. On the cost savings from AI-driven creative production:

> "I built that with one other person over a couple of days... I would have gone to an agency, written a creative brief, probably spent hundreds of thousands, if not a million dollars, to shoot all that creative"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([Semafor, 2025-10-09](https://www.semafor.com/article/10/09/2025/how-jamie-iannone-made-ebay-an-investor-collectible))

---

### AI and the 30-Year Data Moat (2025)

On eBay's competitive advantage from decades of proprietary data that newer entrants like Temu cannot replicate quickly:

> "decades of product listings and customer interactions to train AI models"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([CNN Business, 2025-11-14](https://www.cnn.com/2025/11/14/tech/ebay-ai-shopping))

---

### AI and the Unlisted Inventory Opportunity (2025)

On the scale of addressable market still untapped due to friction in listing — Magical Listing AI is eBay's answer:

> "fewer than 20% of sellable items" [are currently listed on eBay]

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([Semafor, 2025-10-09](https://www.semafor.com/article/10/09/2025/how-jamie-iannone-made-ebay-an-investor-collectible))

---

### Human-to-Human Interaction as a Moat Against Disintermediation (2025)

On why eBay will not be disintermediated by AI agents, even as it embraces agentic commerce:

> "The human-to-human interaction is very important... we don't want AI to take away the ability to find unique products from great sellers and build connections"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([CNN Business, 2025-11-14](https://www.cnn.com/2025/11/14/tech/ebay-ai-shopping))

---

### Crossborder Logistics as a Structural Moat (2022)

On eBay's global seller network as a barrier that new entrants cannot replicate quickly:

> "Over 90% of eBay sellers export internationally versus only 1% of typical SMEs"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([ChannelX, 2022-01](https://channelx.world/2022/01/exclusive-interview-with-jamie-iannone-ebay-ceo/))

---

### Differentiation Strategy Against Temu/Shein (2022)

On maintaining the recommerce and Gen Z strategy under pressure, when eBay's market cap had fallen from $53B to $21B:

> "We're leaning into where Gen Z and millennials are"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([Financial Times via PYMNTS, 2022-10-21](https://www.pymnts.com/news/ecommerce/2022/ebay-bids-on-luxury-recommerce-to-woo-young-shoppers/))

---

### Momentum Confirmation (2025 Year-End)

On eBay's strategic progress at the close of fiscal year 2025:

> "We have built significant momentum across our strategic priorities, delivering meaningful growth and reinforcing our leadership in recommerce"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([Business Chief, 2025](https://businesschief.com/news/ebay-ceo-why-was-2025-a-milestone-for-the-commerce-leader))

> "our results reflect the strength of our strategy and the disciplined execution behind it"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([Business Chief, 2025](https://businesschief.com/news/ebay-ceo-why-was-2025-a-milestone-for-the-commerce-leader))

---

### Early AI Conviction — Supercomputer Investment (2020)

On the countercyclical technology investment during COVID-19, when eBay built its first AI inference supercomputer while competitors contracted:

> "I was a huge believer in AI from the start... we built our first supercomputer for AI inference"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([Northeastern, 2025-10-28](https://news.northeastern.edu/2025/10/28/ebay-ceo-ai-leadership-fireside-chat-northeastern/))

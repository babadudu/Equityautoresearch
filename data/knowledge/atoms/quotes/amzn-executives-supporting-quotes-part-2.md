## Amazon Supporting Executives — CFO Earnings Commentary

This atom collects direct quotes from Amazon CFO Brian Olsavsky from Q4 2025 earnings materials, providing financial context alongside CEO Andy Jassy's strategic commentary.

---

### Brian Olsavsky — CFO

**"Advertising revenue grew 22% in the fourth quarter and we added over $12 billion of incremental revenue in 2025 alone."**
— Brian Olsavsky, CFO, [Q4 2025 Earnings Call](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/05/amazon-amzn-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/)

*Context:* Olsavsky's disclosure puts Amazon's advertising business in stark perspective: $12 billion of incremental advertising revenue added in a single calendar year (2025) approaches roughly one-tenth of AWS's total annualized revenue ($142B). Advertising grew 22% YoY in Q4 2025, maintaining its status as Amazon's highest-margin segment alongside AWS. The combination of AWS (35% operating margin) and advertising (estimated 40%+ contribution margin) is what drove Amazon's overall operating margin to a historic 11.1% in FY2025, compared to 2.4% in FY2022.

*Investment significance:* The $12B incremental advertising figure signals that the Prime Video ad tier launch (mid-2024) and Amazon DSP expansion are generating meaningful new inventory monetization, beyond the core sponsored products business. At $68.6B total advertising revenue for FY2025 (CAGR ~26% from 2020), Amazon has cemented its position as the world's third-largest digital advertising platform behind Google and Meta.

## Jamie Iannone — Seller Partnership, Business Model & Culture Transformation Quotes

### The Core Partnership Principle (2021)

On eBay's fundamental difference from Amazon's retailer-first model — eBay does not compete with its sellers:

> "We don't compete with our sellers. We partner with them. When our sellers succeed, we succeed"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([LinkedIn, 2021](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/empowering-economic-opportunity-all-jamie-iannone))

---

### Sellers as the Core of the Business (2025)

At eBay Open seller conference, on the existential dependency between eBay and its seller community:

> "We all know that we have jobs because of them"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([Semafor, 2025-10-09](https://www.semafor.com/article/10/09/2025/how-jamie-iannone-made-ebay-an-investor-collectible))

---

### Accidental Entrepreneurs (2021)

On sellers who begin by listing items from around the house and evolve into small businesses — the foundation of eBay's C2C-to-SMB pipeline:

> "Sellers like John help power our marketplace, and we innovate with their success in mind"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([LinkedIn, 2021](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/empowering-economic-opportunity-all-jamie-iannone))

On the economic empowerment effect of the platform:

> [sellers] "transform side income into thriving small businesses"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([LinkedIn, 2021](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/empowering-economic-opportunity-all-jamie-iannone))

---

### Sellers as Product Co-Creators (2025)

On the source of eBay's product innovation pipeline — sellers as the best source of ideas:

> "They're on the site 24/7, and they have the best ideas about what could help them"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([ValueAddedResource, 2025](https://www.valueaddedresource.net/ebay-ceo-jamie-iannone-ai-focus-vertial-hype/))

---

### Charity as Community Flywheel (2021)

On eBay for Charity as a tool that increases community stickiness beyond pure commerce:

> "Since 2003... our community has donated more than $1.1 billion through eBay for Charity"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([LinkedIn, 2021](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/empowering-economic-opportunity-all-jamie-iannone))

---

### Consumer Sellers as Dual-Role Value Creators (2020)

At EcommerceBytes, shortly after taking the CEO role, on the underappreciated value of sellers who also become buyers:

> "When people come to sell things around the house, they become twice as valuable as a buyer. We see lots of untapped potential"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([EcommerceBytes, 2020-10-15](https://www.ecommercebytes.com/2020/10/15/ebay-ceo-jamie-iannone-attracted-by-non-new-in-season/))

---

### Vertical-by-Vertical Strategy (2020)

On the go-to-market framework Iannone introduced on Day 1 — deep vertical penetration rather than horizontal breadth:

> "going vertical-by-vertical and saying, 'how do we really win in that market?'"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([EcommerceBytes, 2020-10-15](https://www.ecommercebytes.com/2020/10/15/ebay-ceo-jamie-iannone-attracted-by-non-new-in-season/))

On the headroom in each vertical:

> [eBay] "has low double-digit or high single-digit penetration, and that business is growing double-digits"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([EcommerceBytes, 2020-10-15](https://www.ecommercebytes.com/2020/10/15/ebay-ceo-jamie-iannone-attracted-by-non-new-in-season/))

---

### Sneakers Vertical as Proof of Concept (2021)

At the Goldman Sachs Communacopia conference, on early evidence that the focus category strategy was working:

> "If you think about sneakers, we were doing double digit comps last quarter on the backs of triple digit comps. So really, I think changing the growth opportunity for the business"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([Goldman Sachs Communacopia, 2021-09-21](https://www.valueaddedresource.net/ebay-ceo-jamie-iannone-goldman-sachs-communacopia/))

---

### Managing Seller Change Resistance (2021)

On the structural challenge of introducing platform changes to a seller base that is resistant to any disruption of its workflows:

> "I've known sellers from my eight years in the company before, sellers generally hate change on eBay. And there's always bumpiness as we introduce new features"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([Goldman Sachs Communacopia, 2021-09-21](https://www.valueaddedresource.net/ebay-ceo-jamie-iannone-goldman-sachs-communacopia/))

---

### Managed Payments Integration (2021)

On the completion of the multi-year migration away from PayPal to eBay's own payment system, reducing friction:

> "I don't need to go create a separate account and fund a separate account to buy on eBay. It's now integrated as one single experience"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([Goldman Sachs Communacopia, 2021-09-21](https://www.valueaddedresource.net/ebay-ceo-jamie-iannone-goldman-sachs-communacopia/))

---

### CEO as Active Platform User (undated)

On selling his own Hammond B-3 organ on eBay — Iannone is an active buyer and seller on the platform he leads:

> "On eBay, I sold a Hammond B-3 organ that I've owned since I was a teenager to a church"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([eBayInc.com](https://www.ebayinc.com/company/our-leaders/jamie-iannone/))

### Tech-Led Reimagination — First Quarter as CEO (2020)

On the strategic North Star for eBay's transformation, framed in the first public communication after taking the role:

> [using] "next generation technology, including artificial intelligence and machine learning, to change the experience on eBay"

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO ([EcommerceBytes, 2020-10-15](https://www.ecommercebytes.com/2020/10/15/ebay-ceo-jamie-iannone-attracted-by-non-new-in-season/))

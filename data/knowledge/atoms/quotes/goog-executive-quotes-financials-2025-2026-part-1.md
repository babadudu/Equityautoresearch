# Alphabet CFO & CBO Quotes — Revenue Acceleration and Geographic Growth (2025–2026)

Direct quotes from Alphabet's CFO Anat Ashkenazi and Chief Business Officer Philipp Schindler from earnings calls, with financial context.

---

## Anat Ashkenazi — CFO, Alphabet

### Q4 2025 Earnings Call (February 2026)

**Source:** [Q4 2025 Earnings Call, February 4, 2026](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/04/alphabet-googl-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/)

> "Consolidated revenue of $113.8 billion increased by 18%, or 17% in constant currency."

**Context:** Ashkenazi reporting Q4 2025 results — the quarter in which Alphabet crossed $113.8B in quarterly revenue for the first time. The 18% growth rate accelerated meaningfully versus prior periods, driven by Search, Cloud, and YouTube. Geographic breakdown: US +17%, EMEA +17%, APAC +22%, Other Americas +20%.

## Philipp Schindler — Chief Business Officer, Alphabet

### Q1 2025 Earnings Call (April 2025)

**Source:** [Q1 2025 Earnings Call, April 2025](https://www.rev.com/transcripts/alphabet-q1-2025-earnings-call)

> "The 10% increase in Search and other revenues was led by financial services."

**Context:** Schindler attributing Q1 2025 Search revenue growth (10% YoY) to strength in financial services advertising — insurance, banking, and fintech verticals — which tend to carry higher CPCs (cost-per-click) than retail or entertainment. This signals that AI Overviews did not cannibalize high-value commercial intent queries in the quarter.

## Pierre Omidyar & Jamie Iannone — Recommerce, Seller Neutrality & Platform Founding Quotes

### Context
These quotes span eBay's founding philosophy (Omidyar's feedback system innovation) through to Iannone's contemporary recommerce and seller-neutrality positioning. Together they illustrate the through-line from eBay's 1995 C2C origins to its 2025 strategic identity as a recommerce pioneer that explicitly does not compete with its sellers.

---

### Pierre Omidyar — The Feedback Forum as Platform Foundation

**On the Feedback Forum mechanism (c. 1996):**
> "The feedback forum... allows people to kind of rate each other and give feedback on their transaction."

— Pierre Omidyar, eBay founder, [Academy of Achievement](https://achievement.org/achiever/pierre-omidyar/)

*Note: The Feedback Forum, launched in 1996, was eBay's foundational trust infrastructure — and arguably the first scaled peer-to-peer reputation system on the internet. Omidyar regarded it as one of his most important innovations. This mechanism was subsequently replicated by Amazon Marketplace (seller ratings), Uber/Lyft (rider/driver ratings), Airbnb (host/guest reviews), and effectively became the standard architecture for two-sided marketplace trust. eBay's 30+ years of accumulated feedback data represents a deeply embedded switching cost for long-tenured sellers.*

### Jamie Iannone — Recommerce Pioneer Positioning

**Financial Times interview, 2022 — Leaning into Gen Z and sustainability:**
> "We're leaning into where Gen Z and millennials are... there's a bigger focus on sustainability, and 're-commerce'. eBay is really the pioneer... that gives us an opportunity."

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO, [Financial Times via PYMNTS, 2022-10-21](https://www.pymnts.com/news/ecommerce/2022/ebay-bids-on-luxury-recommerce-to-woo-young-shoppers/)

*Note: Iannone made this statement in 2022, when recommerce was still being framed as a defensive strategy. By FY2025, recommerce (used and refurbished goods) had grown from roughly one-third of GMV to over 40% — validating the strategic bet. The "pioneer" framing is historically accurate: eBay was enabling C2C resale of used goods before "recommerce" existed as a category term.*

**Quantitative context (FY2025):**
- Recommerce > 40% of eBay GMV (FY2025), [eBay Q4 2025 Earnings Call](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/18/ebay-ebay-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/)
- Five-year cumulative positive environmental impact: ~$25 billion equivalent

### Jamie Iannone — Seller Neutrality as Competitive Moat

**LinkedIn post, 2021 — On not competing with sellers:**
> "We don't compete with our sellers."

— Jamie Iannone, eBay CEO, [LinkedIn, 2021](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/empowering-economic-opportunity-all-jamie-iannone)

*Note: This four-word statement carries significant strategic weight in the context of the FTC v. Amazon antitrust case (filed 2023). One of Amazon's core alleged anticompetitive behaviors is using third-party seller data to advantage Amazon's own private-label products — directly competing with the very sellers whose fees fund Amazon's marketplace. eBay has no private-label business and no incentive to cannibalize seller GMV. Iannone's positioning makes this an explicit differentiator, particularly relevant if Amazon is forced by court order or consent decree to reduce its private-label practices (trial scheduled October 2026). Sellers seeking a neutral platform would be the natural beneficiary.*

### Investment Analysis Context

These three quotes — Omidyar on feedback infrastructure, Iannone on recommerce leadership, Iannone on seller neutrality — collectively define eBay's durable competitive identity:

1. **Trust infrastructure** (Omidyar): eBay invented the reputation layer that all two-sided marketplaces now use. Its 30+ year accumulation of feedback data creates a seller and buyer identity moat.

2. **Recommerce first-mover** (Iannone 2022): eBay's C2C heritage means recommerce is not a pivot but a natural identity — competitors (Amazon, Walmart) are playing catch-up in a category eBay has operated since 1995.

3. **Seller alignment** (Iannone 2021): In a regulatory environment increasingly hostile to Amazon's dual-role as marketplace and competitor, eBay's explicit non-competition with sellers is both a trust signal and a regulatory positioning asset.

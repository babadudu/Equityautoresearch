## eBay Category GMV Mix and Focus Categories Performance FY2025

### Strategic Context: Focus Categories

CEO Jamie Iannone's turnaround strategy for eBay, initiated in 2020, centers on concentrating investments in a defined set of high-conviction "focus categories" where eBay can develop differentiated vertical experiences — including authentication, curated inventory, and enthusiast community features. By FY2025, focus categories accounted for approximately **two-thirds of total GMV** ([FinancialContent Analysis, 2026-02-20](https://markets.financialcontent.com/stocks/article/finterra-2026-2-20-resilience-in-re-commerce-an-in-depth-analysis-of-ebay-inc-ebay)).

### Collectibles & Trading Cards: FY2025 Breakout and 2026 Outlook

Trading cards, led by Pokémon, were eBay's standout category in FY2025. The collectibles and trading card vertical expanded to a $10 billion annual business by 2025, with Pokémon cards recording **triple-digit year-over-year growth** ([eBay Q4 2025 Earnings Call, 2026-02-18](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/18/ebay-ebay-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/)).

CFO Steve Alford issued an explicit forward guidance caveat on the Q4 2025 earnings call: "We are expecting a deceleration in Pokémon growth in 2026, just given the triple-digit growth that we saw in 2025. Although, I will point out that we expect healthy overall growth in trading cards" ([eBay Q4 2025 Earnings Call, 2026-02-18](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/18/ebay-ebay-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/)). This signals that eBay's management views 2025 Pokémon performance as a high watermark unlikely to repeat at the same rate, while remaining confident in the broader trading card vertical's structural growth.

### Electronics and Fashion: Re-Commerce Leaders

Electronics (28% of GMV) and Fashion (17%) are the two largest categories by volume and represent eBay's strongest re-commerce positioning:
- **Refurbished electronics** grew 18% YoY, supported by the eBay Refurbished certification program that provides buyers with condition guarantees and seller accountability.
- **Pre-owned fashion** grew 22% YoY, with certified handbags serving as a marquee authenticated luxury segment.

### Auto Parts: Structural Moat

Auto Parts (12% GMV share) represents one of eBay's most defensible verticals. The category features over **400 million active parts listings** and generates more than **$10 billion in GMV annually**, served by **40 million buyers** ([LinkedIn, 2021](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/empowering-economic-opportunity-all-jamie-iannone)). The 2024 acquisition of Caramel extended eBay's addressable market within Motors to include full vehicle peer-to-peer transactions, complementing the established parts and accessories business.

### Key Takeaways

1. **Focus categories = ~two-thirds of GMV**: Iannone's vertical concentration strategy has delivered meaningful share of platform activity in authenticated, curated verticals.
2. **Trading cards are a 2025 tailwind with a 2026 base effect headwind**: Triple-digit Pokémon growth in 2025 sets a high comparison base; management guides for deceleration but continued healthy category growth.
3. **Re-commerce is eBay's differentiating theme**: Refurbished electronics (+18%) and pre-owned fashion (+22%) YoY growth rates significantly outpace broader e-commerce market growth rates.
4. **Auto Parts is a structural moat**: 400M listings, $10B+ GMV, 40M buyers — scale and catalogue depth make this category difficult for generalist competitors to replicate.
5. **Luxury authentication** (5% of GMV across handbags, sneakers, watches) is a high-value, low-volume segment designed to attract affluent buyers and reduce counterfeit risk.

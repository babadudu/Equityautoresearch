### Key Observations

**Tirzepatide dominance**: The combined Mounjaro + Zepbound franchise reached $36.5B in 2025, representing 56% of total company revenue. Lilly's revenue profile has shifted to be decisively dependent on a single molecule (tirzepatide) across two indications.

**Trulicity decline**: The legacy GLP-1 agonist Trulicity (dulaglutide) declined -26% in 2024 as patients and physicians migrated to the superior efficacy of tirzepatide. This cannibalization was anticipated and managed, but it represents a structural product lifecycle transition.

**Verzenio as secondary anchor**: The CDK4/6 inhibitor Verzenio grew +37% in 2024 and +8% in 2025, providing meaningful revenue diversification outside the GLP-1 franchise at $5.7B in 2025 (8.7% of revenue).

**International acceleration in Q4 2025**: International segment growth reached +43% in Q4 2025, outpacing US growth, signaling that the geographic mix shift away from US concentration is underway.

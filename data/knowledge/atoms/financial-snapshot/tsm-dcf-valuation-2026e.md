## TSMC DCF Valuation & Scenario Analysis (2026E)

**Data vintage:** Q4 2025 earnings / January 2026. Refresh by: April 2026.

---

### Guidance Anchors

TSMC's Q4 2025 earnings call provided explicit 2026 guidance:
- **Q1 2026 revenue guidance:** USD $34.6–35.8B
- **Full-year 2026 revenue growth:** ~+30% YoY in USD terms
- **Q1 2026 gross margin guidance:** 63–65%
- **2026E CapEx guidance:** $52–56B

Base year: 2025 actual revenue ~$115B (NT basis ~$2.87T).

Sources: [TSMC Q4 2025 Transcript](https://investor.tsmc.com/english/encrypt/files/encrypt_file/reports/2026-01/51d09df96cd89ac19d65af39032b038dc2896a24/TSMC%204Q25%20Transcript.pdf); [TSMC Q4 2025 Earnings](https://investor.tsmc.com/english/quarterly-results/2025/q4)

CEO C.C. Wei on demand confidence: *"I spend a lot of time in the last three to four months talking to my customers and end customers' customer. I want to make sure that my customers' demand is real."* ([The Next Platform, 2026-01-16](https://www.nextplatform.com/compute/2026/01/16/tsmc-has-no-choice-but-to-trust-the-sunny-ai-forecasts-of-its-customers/4092173))

CFO Wendell Huang on capital efficiency: *"If we do our job right, the growth of our business should outpace the growth of the CapEx."* He also noted: *"In the last three years, our capex dollars amount totaled $101 billion, but is expected to be significantly higher in the next three years."* ([The Next Platform, 2026-01-16](https://www.nextplatform.com/compute/2026/01/16/tsmc-has-no-choice-but-to-trust-the-sunny-ai-forecasts-of-its-customers/4092173); [TSMC Q3 2025 Transcript](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/10/17/tsmc-tsm-q3-2025-earnings-call-transcript/))

**Base case conclusion:** At 2026E EPS NT$439 and 25x P/E, target price is approximately NT$10,975. Three-year annualized IRR ~18%.

**Key risks (downside):**
- Taiwan Strait geopolitical escalation → P/E compression below 20x
- Offshore fab cost overruns → net margin compression of 2–3pp

**Key opportunities (upside):**
- AI inference demand inflection faster than expected → HPC revenue share reaches 60%+
- N2 node ramp yield outperformance → gross margin exceeds 63%

### Key Metrics Summary

**Revenue trajectory:**
- 2014 trough: $19.62B (post-Zyprexa patent cliff)
- 2025 peak: $65.18B (+232% over 11 years)
- 2024→2025 acceleration: +45% YoY, the fastest single-year growth in the period

**Operating margin trajectory:**
- 2015 floor: 14.0% (patent cliff nadir)
- 2017 anomaly: 11.5% (tax reform one-time charge distortion)
- 2020 interim peak: 29.5% (COVID antibody contribution)
- 2023 dip: 19.2% (heavy R&D and manufacturing investment ahead of GLP-1 ramp)
- 2025 peak: 39.5% (full GLP-1 operating leverage)

**EPS trajectory:**
- Only negative year: 2017 (-$0.26, tax reform charge)
- Artificially elevated year: 2019 ($8.57, Elanco spinoff one-time gains)
- 2025 normalized EPS: $22.95 (~90x recovery from 2017 trough on underlying operations)

**Operating leverage signal (2024→2025):**
- Revenue grew +45% ($45.04B → $65.18B)
- Operating income grew +103% ($12.69B → $25.73B)
- Incremental revenue fell almost entirely to the bottom line, confirming high-fixed-cost manufacturing leverage at scale

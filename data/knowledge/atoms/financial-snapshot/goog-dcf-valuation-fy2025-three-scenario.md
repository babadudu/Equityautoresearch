### Key Risks to DCF Assumptions

1. **AI CapEx overhang:** 2025 CapEx guidance $75B (up from ~$25B in FY2024 estimate used above) could compress FCF materially beyond the bear case if revenue growth disappoints.
2. **Antitrust outcomes:** DOJ search distribution case and potential remedies (e.g., forced Chrome divestiture, default search agreement bans) could structurally impair Search revenue — not fully reflected in bear case.
3. **Cloud monetization pace:** $155B backlog conversion depends on enterprise migration timelines; slower ramp delays terminal FCF.
4. **Multiple compression:** If AI-native competitors (OpenAI, Perplexity) erode Search's perceived moat, market may reprice Alphabet closer to value multiples rather than growth multiples.
5. **Macro sensitivity:** Ad spending is cyclical; a recession scenario would compress Search and YouTube revenues simultaneously.

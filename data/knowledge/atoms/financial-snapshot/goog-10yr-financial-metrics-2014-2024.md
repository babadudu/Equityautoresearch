## Alphabet (GOOG) 10-Year Core Financial Metrics 2014–2024

The following table covers Alphabet's core financials from 2014 (final year of Google Inc.) through 2024 (AI acceleration year). Revenue and net income figures are sourced from Alphabet's annual 10-K filings and Q4 earnings press releases, cross-validated against [Stock Analysis](https://stockanalysis.com/stocks/googl/financials/) and [BusinessTats](https://businesstats.com/alphabet-global-annual-revenue/).

| Year | Revenue ($B) | YoY% | Gross Margin | Operating Income ($B) | Operating Margin | Net Income ($B) | Diluted EPS | Notes |
|------|-------------|------|-------------|----------------------|-----------------|----------------|-------------|-------|
| 2014 | 66.0 | +19% | 61% | 16.5 | 25% | 14.4 | $2.07 | Final year as Google Inc. |
| 2015 | 75.0 | +14% | 62% | 19.4 | 26% | 16.3 | $2.34 | **Alphabet restructuring**; Pichai named Google CEO |
| 2016 | 90.3 | +20% | 61% | 23.7 | 26% | 19.5 | $2.79 | Accelerated Cloud infrastructure investment |
| 2017 | 110.9 | +23% | 59% | 26.1 | 24% | 12.6 | $1.83 | EU Shopping fine €2.4B compressed net income |
| 2018 | 136.8 | +23% | 56% | 27.5 | 20% | 30.7 | $4.44 | EU Android fine €4.3B; YouTube revenue acceleration |
| 2019 | 161.9 | +18% | 56% | 34.2 | 21% | 34.3 | $4.96 | **Cloud revenue first disclosed at $8.9B**; Pichai assumes Alphabet CEO role |
| 2020 | 182.5 | +13% | 54% | 41.2 | 23% | 40.3 | $5.86 | **COVID V-shaped recovery**; Q2 first-ever YoY revenue decline, strong Q3–Q4 rebound |
| 2021 | 257.6 | +41% | 57% | 78.7 | 31% | 76.0 | $5.61 | **Post-COVID ad boom**; revenue grew $75.1B in one year; operating margin expanded 23%→31% |
| 2022 | 282.8 | +10% | 55% | 74.8 | 26% | 60.0 | $4.56 | Ad slowdown + headcount bloat (190K employees) + rising interest rate impact |
| 2023 | 307.4 | +9% | 57% | 84.3 | 27% | 73.8 | $5.80 | **12,000 layoffs + efficiency year**; Cloud first full-year profit of $1.7B |
| 2024 | 350.0 | +14% | 58% | 112.4 | 32% | 100.1 | $8.04 | **AI year**: Cloud +31%=$43.2B; net income first crossed $100B |

*Sources: [Alphabet 10-K 2024](https://s206.q4cdn.com/479360582/files/doc_financials/2024/q4/goog-10-k-2024.pdf), [Alphabet Q4 2024 Press Release](https://abc.xyz/assets/17/22/5e7e42e0455dbf46db1c8a3a822a/goog-exhibit-991-q4-2024.pdf), [Stock Analysis](https://stockanalysis.com/stocks/googl/financials/), [BusinessTats](https://businesstats.com/alphabet-global-annual-revenue/). EPS is diluted, adjusted for the 20-for-1 stock split in July 2022.*

## Summary Metrics

- **Revenue CAGR (2014–2024):** ~16.4% ($66.0B → $350.0B)
- **Net Income CAGR (2014–2024):** ~21.3% ($14.4B → $100.1B)
- **Peak operating margin:** 32% (2024, historical high)
- **Trough operating margin:** 20% (2018, EU fine cycle)
- **2024 milestones:** Net income crossed $100B for the first time; operating income $112.4B; Cloud operating income $6.1B (14.1% margin) vs. $1.7B in 2023

## Google Cloud Profitability Trajectory

Cloud operating income/loss progression (representing the "second growth curve" validation):

| Year | Cloud Operating Income ($B) |
|------|----------------------------|
| 2020 | -5.6 |
| 2021 | -3.1 |
| 2022 | -3.0 |
| 2023 | +1.7 |
| 2024 | +6.1 |

The shift from deep losses to double-digit margins over five years is the primary financial validation of Alphabet's Cloud bet and directly supports management's $75B 2025 CapEx guidance.

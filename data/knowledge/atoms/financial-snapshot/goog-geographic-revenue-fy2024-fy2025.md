## Alphabet (GOOG) Geographic Revenue — FY2024 to FY2025

### Overview

Alphabet reported total revenue of $403.0 billion in FY2025, up +15.2% year-over-year from $349.8 billion in FY2024. Revenue is reported across four geographic segments: United States, EMEA (Europe, Middle East & Africa), APAC (Asia-Pacific), and Other Americas.

### Geographic Revenue Table

### Key Observations

**APAC is the fastest-growing region** at +19.1% YoY, driven by accelerating digital advertising penetration in India and Southeast Asia, as well as Cloud AI demand. APAC's share of total revenue increased from 16.2% in FY2024 to 16.8% in FY2025, the only region to gain meaningful share.

**US remains the dominant segment** at ~48% of revenue, growing +14.0% — the slowest of the four regions, consistent with its relative market maturity.

**EMEA and Other Americas** grew at +14.7% and +17.1% respectively, both slightly above US growth, with EMEA maintaining its ~29% share.

### Q4 2025 Acceleration

Anat Ashkenazi (CFO) confirmed broad-based geographic acceleration in the Q4 2025 earnings call: "Consolidated revenue of $113.8 billion increased by 18%, or 17% in constant currency." In Q4 2025 specifically, the US and EMEA each grew +17%, APAC grew +22%, and Other Americas grew +20%. ([Q4 2025 Earnings Call, Feb 2026](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/04/alphabet-googl-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/))

Philipp Schindler (Chief Business Officer) highlighted Search strength across regions in Q1 2025: "The 10% increase in Search and other revenues was led by financial services." ([Q1 2025 Earnings Call, Apr 2025](https://www.rev.com/transcripts/alphabet-q1-2025-earnings-call))

### India & APAC Strategic Significance

Sundar Pichai has explicitly positioned India as a core pillar of Alphabet's global AI strategy. At the India AI Impact Summit (February 2026), Pichai announced a $15 billion investment commitment: "We have invested $15 billion in India's digital infrastructure over the past five years, and we are just getting started." He further stated: "India is uniquely positioned to lead in every layer of the AI stack — from foundational research to real-world applications." ([India AI Impact Summit, Feb 2026](https://singjupost.com/sundar-pichai-remarks-india-ai-impact-summit-2026/))

This investment underscores why APAC is the fastest-growing geographic segment and is expected to continue outpacing other regions as digital ad penetration in India/Southeast Asia is still in early stages relative to the US and Western Europe.

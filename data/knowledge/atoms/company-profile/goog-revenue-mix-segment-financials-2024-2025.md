### AI-Driven Evolution of Advertising Revenue

The core commercial logic of search advertising is intent monetization—queries entered into the search box directly reflect purchase intent, and advertisers bid accordingly. AI is deepening this logic across three dimensions.

**1. AI Overviews expands rather than cannibalizes search monetization.** Pichai at Q1 2025 earnings: "AI Overviews is going very well with over 1.5 billion users per month"—and further emphasized AI is driving more commercial queries: "Volume of commercial queries has increased." ([Q1 2025 Earnings Call, Apr 2025](https://www.rev.com/transcripts/alphabet-q1-2025-earnings-call))

CFO Anat Ashkenazi at the same call documented the leap in Cloud profitability from scale: "Operating margin increased from 9.4% to 17.8%." ([Q1 2025 Earnings Call, Apr 2025](https://www.rev.com/transcripts/alphabet-q1-2025-earnings-call))

**2. AI-powered ad tools are improving advertiser ROI.** Philipp Schindler (CBO) at Q2 2025: "Advertisers that activate AI Max in search campaigns typically see 14% more conversions" and "Campaigns using Smart Bidding Exploration see a 19% increase in conversions on average." ([Q2 2025 Earnings Call, Jul 2025](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/07/23/alphabet-googl-q2-2025-earnings-call-transcript/))

At Q1 2025, Schindler revealed Demand Gen efficacy: "Businesses using Demand Gen now see an average 26% year-on-year increase in conversions per dollar spent for goals like purchases and leads. And when using Demand Gen with product feed, on average they see more than double the conversion per dollar spent year-over-year." ([Q1 2025 Earnings Call, Apr 2025](https://ppc.land/google-q1-2025-earnings-reveal-advertising-strength-amid-ai-transformation/))

At Q1 2024, Schindler had previewed this trajectory: "As advances accelerate in our underlying AI models, our ability to help businesses find users at speed and scale and drive ROI just keeps getting better." ([Q1 2024 Earnings Call, Apr 2024](https://blog.google/inside-google/message-ceo/alphabet-earnings-q1-2024/))

**3. AI search monetization is evolving from keyword→ad to conversation→native checkout.** At NRF 2026, Pichai unveiled Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP): "[UCP] was built to meet the needs of retailers and customers, keeping the full customer relationship front and centre from discovery and decision through to checkout and beyond." The closed loop of buyer advertising → AI search → native checkout, if successful, would lift search ARPU. ([NRF 2026 Keynote, Jan 2026](https://blog.google/company-news/inside-google/message-ceo/nrf-2026-remarks/))

### Cloud: From Loss to Profit Engine

Cloud's business model is transitioning from "infrastructure rental" to "AI platform monetization."

Ashkenazi at Q4 2025 described the inflection: "Cloud operating income was $5.3 billion more than doubling year over year." Operating margin surged from 17.5% to 30.1%: "Operating margin increased from 17.5% in the fourth quarter of last year to 30.1%." Backlog doubled: "Google Cloud's backlog increased 55% sequentially and more than doubled year over year, reaching $240 billion." ([Q4 2025 Earnings Call, Feb 2026](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/04/alphabet-googl-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/))

Early signs appeared in Q2 2025—Ashkenazi reported: "Google Cloud operating margin increased from 11.3% to 20.7%." ([Q2 2025 Earnings Call, Jul 2025](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/07/23/alphabet-googl-q2-2025-earnings-call-transcript/))

Q2 2025 backlog context: "Google Cloud backlog increased 18% sequentially in Q2 and 38% year over year, reaching $106 billion." ([Q2 2025 Earnings Call, Jul 2025](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/07/23/alphabet-googl-q2-2025-earnings-call-transcript/))

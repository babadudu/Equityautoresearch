## Agentic Commerce: Market Sizing, Infrastructure Inflection, and eBay's Positioning (2025–2030)

Agentic commerce—AI agents autonomously executing shopping tasks including search, comparison, and purchase—is transitioning from the innovator stage to early adopter. The category is experiencing explosive growth from a small base and is approaching a structural inflection point as competing protocols from Google and OpenAI mature.

### Market Sizing

The global agentic commerce market grew from $178M in 2021 to **$547M in 2025**, with projections reaching **$5.2B by 2033** (CAGR 32.5%) ([Sanbi.ai Agentic Commerce Market Report 2026](https://sanbi.ai/blog/agentic-shopping-market-trends)). The broader agentic AI ecosystem—encompassing all industries—grows from $7.29B in 2025 to $139.19B in 2034 (CAGR 40.5%) ([Fortune Business Insights](https://www.fortunebusinessinsights.com/agentic-ai-market-114233)).

Morgan Stanley projects that by 2030, agentic-driven spending in US e-commerce alone will reach **$190–385 billion** (10–20% of US e-commerce), with global scale potentially reaching $3–5 trillion ([Morgan Stanley Agentic Commerce Outlook](https://www.morganstanley.com/insights/articles/agentic-commerce-market-impact-outlook)).

### Current Adoption Signals

- During Cyber Week 2025, **20% of global online orders** were influenced by AI agents or shopping assistants (Salesforce data)
- AI-sourced retail website traffic grew YoY by **1,200%** during the same period ([Sanbi.ai](https://sanbi.ai/blog/agentic-shopping-market-trends))
- 60% of US consumers expect to use an AI shopping agent within 12 months ([Sanbi.ai](https://sanbi.ai/blog/agentic-shopping-market-trends))
- By 2030, analysts project **20–30% of all online transactions** will involve AI agent intermediation at some stage ([Sanbi.ai](https://sanbi.ai/blog/agentic-shopping-market-trends))
- GenAI-sourced traffic converted at **31% higher rates** than traditional channels ([Adobe Analytics via Envive.ai](https://www.envive.ai/post/generative-ai-commerce-adoption-statistics))

### The January 2026 Infrastructure Inflection Point

In January 2026, three major technology platforms simultaneously launched agentic commerce infrastructure, marking a structural transition in the ecosystem:

1. **Google** launched Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP), partnering with Walmart to enable products to be discovered and purchased through Gemini
2. **Microsoft** launched Brand Agents and personalized shopping agent templates, with Guess as an early brand partner
3. **OpenAI** expanded Instant Checkout to support multi-item shopping carts, with Etsy, Shopify, and Walmart as partners—**over 1 million Shopify merchants joined OpenAI Instant Checkout**

Sources: [Sanbi.ai](https://sanbi.ai/blog/agentic-shopping-market-trends); [commercetools AI Trends 2026](https://commercetools.com/blog/ai-trends-shaping-agentic-commerce)

The parallel development of two competing protocols—OpenAI ACP (Agent Commerce Protocol) and Google UCP—signals that 2026–2027 is the **critical inflection window** for agentic commerce infrastructure. Once either protocol achieves dominant adoption, AI agents will be able to autonomously complete the full workflow from discovery to checkout, reshaping the discoverability dynamics for all e-commerce platforms.

### eBay's Positioning in Agentic Commerce

eBay is one of the earliest e-commerce platforms to deploy agentic commerce. Its OpenAI Operator partnership went live in January 2025, enabling AI agents to act autonomously on behalf of buyers within eBay's marketplace. Iannone stated the strategic rationale: "This agentic approach to ecommerce could create an entirely new channel for discovery and shopping online" ([Digital Commerce 360, 2025-03-10](https://www.digitalcommerce360.com/2025/03/10/ebay-sales-revenue-gmv-q4-2024/)).

Furthermore, eBay has built in-house hyper-optimized LLMs to execute specific shopping agent tasks at lower latency than general-purpose models, and has integrated agentic capabilities "into the core of eBay Inc.'s business through the main search experience" ([eBay Q3 2025 Earnings Call, 2025-10-29](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/10/29/ebay-ebay-q3-2025-earnings-call-transcript/)).

**eBay's structural advantages in agentic commerce:**
- 2.4 billion active listings provide AI agents with unmatched inventory depth, particularly in long-tail categories (collectibles, parts, refurbished electronics)
- 30 years of transaction data constitutes a proprietary training dataset for shopping-specific LLMs
- Early mover status with OpenAI Operator provides integration depth ahead of competitors

**Risk: Protocol dependency**

As Google UCP and OpenAI ACP become mainstream, platform discoverability will partially shift from search engines to AI agent protocols. eBay must ensure its inventory is prioritized in major AI agent indexes. Platforms that are not well-integrated with dominant agentic protocols risk losing the discovery funnel—a structural risk given eBay's historical SEO dependence on Google. Iannone's move to embed agentic capabilities into eBay's core search experience is a direct response to this discoverability risk.

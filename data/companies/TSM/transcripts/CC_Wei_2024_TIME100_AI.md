## Profile Key Facts
- Wei named CEO since 2018, Chairman since June 2024
- TSMC is the only company globally capable of fabricating cutting-edge chips powering advanced AI
- Market value nearly tripled over Wei's six-year tenure as CEO
- Clients include Apple, NVIDIA, and AMD
- Initiated significant global expansion: US, Germany, Japan
- Received over $6 billion in CHIPS Act subsidies for Arizona
- Wei emphasizes recruitment and capability building in Western markets

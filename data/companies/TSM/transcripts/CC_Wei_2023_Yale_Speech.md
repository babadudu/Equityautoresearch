# CC Wei — Yale Science Building Speech
**Date**: 2023-10-05
**Source**: [Yale Daily News](https://yaledailynews.com/blog/2023/10/05/taiwan-semiconductor-ceo-c-c-wei-speaks-at-the-yale-science-building/)

## Key Quotes

"The U.S. is the country with the most talented and innovative people, especially for the Ivy League student."
— On American talent advantage, delivered at his alma mater.

## Context
Wei returned to Yale University (where he earned his PhD in Electrical Engineering in 1985 under Professor Tso-Ping Ma) to speak at the Yale Science Building. He discussed TSMC's Arizona fab selection, noting the US lacks sufficient on-site semiconductor engineers compared to Taiwan, which is why TSMC chose Arizona for its relatively larger technical talent pool. The speech reflected Wei's personal connection to American higher education and his nuanced view of US manufacturing challenges — admiring American innovation while acknowledging the structural disadvantages in semiconductor fabrication workforce compared to Taiwan's concentrated engineering ecosystem.

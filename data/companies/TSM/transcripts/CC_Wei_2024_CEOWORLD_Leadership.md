# CC Wei — Dual Leadership Ascension at TSMC
**Date**: 2024-06-04
**Source**: [CEOWORLD Magazine](https://ceoworld.biz/2024/06/04/ceo-c-c-wei-ascends-to-dual-leadership-roles-at-tsmc/)

## Key Quotes & Data Points

At TSMC's 2024 shareholders meeting, Wei projected:
- "Up to 30% year-on-year revenue increase for Q2" driven by strong AI processor demand
- Full-year revenue growth anticipated "between 20% and 25%"

On overseas costs (October 2023): "higher initial costs...compared to domestic operations"
— Acknowledging the structural cost disadvantage of non-Taiwan fabs.

## Context
Wei assumed the Chairman role following Mark Liu's departure, making him the first person since Morris Chang to hold both CEO and Chairman positions. The reorganization aimed to groom a successor for the CEO position. At the time, Q4 2023 net income had fallen 19.3% to $7.4B, and 2023 CapEx was cut 16.3% to $30.4B — but Wei was already signaling the AI-driven recovery ahead.

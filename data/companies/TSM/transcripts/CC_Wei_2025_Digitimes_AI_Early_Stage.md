# CC Wei — AI Still in Early Stage
**Date**: November 24, 2025
**Source**: [Digitimes](https://www.digitimes.com/news/a20251124VL206/taiwan-tsmc-chairman-intel-arizona.html)
**Context**: Industry conference remarks
**Format**: Public statements and media coverage

---

## AI Development Assessment

Wei's key declaration:
> AI development is "still in early stage."

This statement was significant because it came after TSMC had already seen:
- 2024 AI accelerator revenue tripling
- 2025 AI accelerator revenue doubling again
- Five consecutive quarters of AI demand exceeding expectations

Yet Wei maintained the growth cycle was just beginning — implying multiple years of structural demand ahead.

---

## US Expansion

Wei also discussed TSMC's continued expansion in Arizona, emphasizing the strategic importance of geographic diversification while maintaining technology leadership in Taiwan.

---

## Context

This remark became a touchstone for bull-case analysts:
- If AI is "still in early stage" after 2 years of explosive growth, the total addressable demand runway extends well beyond current consensus estimates
- Wei's credibility is enhanced by his 2-3 year forward visibility into customer roadmaps
- Contrasts with market concerns about AI CapEx overinvestment

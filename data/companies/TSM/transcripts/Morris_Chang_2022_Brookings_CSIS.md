# Morris Chang — Brookings Institution & CSIS Interview
**Date**: April 2022
**Source**: [Brookings Institution — Vying for Talent](https://www.brookings.edu/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/Vying-for-Talent-Morris-Chang-20220414.pdf)
**Context**: Rare interview on semiconductor geopolitics, talent, and US manufacturing

## Key Quotes

### Globalization & Free Trade
- "Globalization is almost dead. Free trade is almost dead."

### US Semiconductor Manufacturing
- Called the US effort to bolster domestic semiconductor manufacturing "a very expensive exercise in futility."

### Identity
- "My identity has always been an American."

### TSMC & Taiwan
- Emphasized Taiwan's irreplaceable manufacturing ecosystem advantages over the US
- Discussed the structural challenges of replicating TSMC's manufacturing culture overseas

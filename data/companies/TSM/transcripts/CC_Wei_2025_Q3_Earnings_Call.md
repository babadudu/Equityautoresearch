# TSMC Q3 2025 Earnings Call Transcript
**Date**: October 17, 2025
**Source**: [Motley Fool Transcript](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/10/17/tsmc-tsm-q3-2025-earnings-call-transcript/) | [TSMC IR PDF](https://investor.tsmc.com/english/encrypt/files/encrypt_file/reports/2025-10/6860312f04fd291d0f26b46c1234f84e6332717e/TSMC%203Q25%20Transcript.pdf)
**Speakers**: C.C. Wei (Chairman & CEO), Wendell Huang (VP & CFO)

---

## Opening Remarks

### Wendell Huang (CFO)
Third quarter revenue: $33.1 billion, exceeding guidance. Gross margin: 59.5%, up 0.9 percentage points due to cost improvements and capacity utilization gains.

### C.C. Wei (CEO)
Management observed "very strong" AI-related demand throughout 2025.

---

## AI Demand & Customer Engagement

> "AI demand has continued to be very strong and stronger than expected three months prior."

> "AI demand actually continue to be very strong, it's more — more stronger than we thought 3 months ago. The numbers are insane."

> "The number of tokens increase is exponential. Almost every 3 months, it will exponentially increase."

> "demand on leading edge semiconductor is real"

On demand verification methodology:
> "Right now, we pay a lot of attention to our customers' customer. We talk with them and see how they view the AI application, and then we make a judgment about how AI is going to grow."

> "we directly received very strong signals from our customers' customers, requesting the capacity"

> "As process technology complexity increases, the engagement lead time with customer is now at least 2 to 3 years in advance. Therefore, we probably get the deepest and widest look possible in the industry."

> "That's why we are still very comfortable that the demand on leading edge semiconductor is real."

---

## China Market & Export Controls

## N2 Technology & Product Family

> "N2 is well on track for volume production later this quarter."

> "We believe N2, N2P, A16 and its derivatives will propel our N2 family to be another large and long-lasting node."

## Capacity Planning

> "we work very closely with our customers and our customers' customer to plan our capacity"

> "Everything related like front-end and back-end capacity is very tight. We are working very hard to make sure that the gap will be narrow."

> "Advanced-node wafer demand is currently about three times short of available capacity."

---

## CapEx & Investment Philosophy (Huang)

Full-year 2025 CapEx narrowed to $40-42 billion (from $38-42 billion). Approximately 70% advanced processes; 10-20% specialty; 10-20% advanced packaging.

> "A higher level of capital expenditures is always going to be correlated with higher growth opportunities in the following years. As long as we believe there are business opportunities, we will not hesitate to invest."

> "If we do our job right, the growth of our business should outpace the growth of the CapEx."

Overseas fab dilution revised downward to 1-2% for 2025 (from 2-3% previously).

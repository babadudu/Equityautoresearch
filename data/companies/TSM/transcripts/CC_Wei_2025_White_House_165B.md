# CC Wei — White House $165B US Investment Announcement
**Date**: March 2025
**Source**: [TSMC PR](https://pr.tsmc.com/english/news/3210) | [TIME](https://time.com/collections/time100-ai-2025/7305839/cc-wei-ai/)
**Context**: Joint announcement with President Trump at the White House
**Format**: Press conference and official statement

---

## Announcement

TSMC announced expansion of total US investment to $165 billion, the largest foreign direct investment in US history. The plan includes:
- 6 wafer fabs in Arizona (4nm → 2nm)
- 2 advanced packaging facilities
- 1 R&D center
- Target: 30,000+ jobs

---

## Key Quotes

On AI and semiconductor's role:
> "AI is reshaping our daily lives, and semiconductor technology is the foundation for new capabilities and applications."

On customer-driven expansion:
> "Many customers stress the importance of TSMC's manufacturing expertise with geographic flexibility."

---

## Context

This announcement was politically significant:
1. Came amid Trump tariff threats against semiconductor imports
2. Largest FDI commitment by a single company in US history
3. Wei stood alongside Trump — a diplomatic milestone for TSMC
4. Signaled TSMC's willingness to invest massively to maintain US government goodwill
5. Arizona first fab already in high-volume production with "yield comparable to Taiwan fabs"

Wei later reflected on this moment in Fortune Asia:
> "If someone had told me 10 years ago that I'd meet two presidents in one week to discuss hundreds of billions of dollars in investments to expand our global footprint, I would have thought they were joking."
